# AI instructions

This is a Cloudflare Pages website for a lawyer/law firm.

Goal:
Make the site premium, modern, trustworthy, professional, and conversion-focused so visitors want to contact and hire the lawyer.

Rules:
- Preserve the real lawyer name, phone number, email, address, links, forms, practice areas, and factual copy.
- Do not invent awards, testimonials, settlements, verdicts, years of experience, guarantees, or bar memberships.
- Do not say "guaranteed win."
- Do not say "best lawyer" or "#1 lawyer" unless already present in the original content.
- Keep the site deployable on Cloudflare Pages.
- If plain HTML/CSS/JS, keep it static and lightweight.
- Improve mobile responsiveness, SEO metadata, accessibility, visual hierarchy, and page speed.

Design direction:
Premium law firm design with strong hero section, clear calls to action, click-to-call phone links, practice area cards, attorney profile section, contact section, refined typography, dark navy/charcoal/white/gold palette, and strong footer.
