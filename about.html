About Amanda

Experience in mediation, paralegal work, and client-focused support.

Amanda brings conflict-resolution training, domestic mediation experience, paralegal education, and LPP licensing to help Utah clients through difficult legal moments.

Who I am

Amanda earned a Conflict Resolution certificate from the University of Utah in 2014, completed the domestic program at UDR, interned under a master mediator, and has been on the Court Roster for Domestic Mediators since 2016.

She earned an Associate of Applied Science Degree in Paralegal Studies in 2022, passed the LPP Bar exam in April 2024, and was sworn in May 2024.

Amanda is passionate about helping people who may otherwise go unrepresented because of limited means.
