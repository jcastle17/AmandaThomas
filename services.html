Services

Focused help across family law, debt collection, and mediation.

These areas are organized around the services Amanda currently provides as a Utah Licensed Paralegal Practitioner and mediator.

My daughter’s slogan she said I had to use: “I do law stuff.”

Divorce

Navigate divorce with clarity and reduced stress. We help you understand the process, organize essential paperwork, and move toward your next chapter with confidence.

Debt Collection

Seeking assistance with debt collection matters within Utah's small claims limit? We provide clear guidance and support through the process.

Mediation

Reach mutually agreeable resolutions through mediation. As a neutral third party, Amanda facilitates constructive dialogue, empowering you to control the outcome of your dispute.

Temporary Separation

Secure essential needs during legal proceedings. We assist with temporary orders for custody, support, and other critical matters while your case is pending.

Cohabitation Abuse

Seeking protection from abuse or harassment? We offer assistance with protective orders and related matters for cohabitants facing fear or harm.

Civil Stalking

Navigating civil stalking concerns? We help you understand Utah law and prepare the necessary materials to address a concerning pattern of conduct.

Custody / Parentage

Prioritize your children's well-being. We provide support for custody, parent-time, child support, and parentage cases, focusing on maintaining strong parent-child relationships.

Relationship Recognized as Marriage

Need to formalize a recognized relationship as a marriage? We assist with petitions that meet Utah's specific timing and filing requirements.

Name Changes

Streamline your legal name change. We guide you through petition requirements, notice procedures, and court preparation.

Modifications

Circumstances change. We help you prepare and file requests to modify existing orders for child support, custody, alimony, or spousal support.

Child Support

Ensure fairness and accuracy in child support matters. We help you understand Utah's guidelines and prepare the necessary documentation.

Gender Marker Changes

Guide your legal gender marker change. We provide assistance with the petition process, eligibility, and verification requirements.
