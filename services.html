Services

Focused help across family law, debt collection, and mediation.

These areas are organized around the services Amanda currently provides as a Utah Licensed Paralegal Practitioner and mediator.

My daughter’s slogan she said I had to use: “I do law stuff.”

Divorce

Divorce is emotional and stressful. The goal is to help clients understand the process, organize the paperwork, and move forward with less fear.

Debt Collection

Help with debt collection matters where the amount at issue falls within the statutory limit for small claims cases.

Mediation

A neutral process where a mediator helps parties work toward a mutually acceptable resolution while maintaining control over the final agreement.

Temporary Separation

Temporary orders can address important issues while a divorce or modification case is pending and before the final order is entered.

Cohabitation Abuse

Assistance with qualifying protective-order-related matters involving cohabitants and allegations of abuse or fear of imminent physical harm.

Civil Stalking

Help understanding and preparing civil stalking materials where a course of conduct is directed at a specific person under Utah law.

Custody / Parentage

Support for child custody, parent-time, child support, and parentage cases, with an emphasis on best interests and close continuing parent-child relationships.

Relationship Recognized as Marriage

Help with petitions asking the court to recognize a qualifying relationship as a marriage, subject to Utah timing and filing requirements.

Name Changes

Assistance with name-change petitions, notice requirements, and preparation for the court process.

Modifications

Help with requests to modify existing orders involving child support, custody, parent-time, alimony, or spousal support when circumstances have changed.

Child Support

Help understanding Utah child support guidelines and preparing the documents needed for child support issues.

Gender Marker Changes

Guidance with the petition process for changing a gender marker, including basic eligibility and required verification steps.
